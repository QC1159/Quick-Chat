Quick Chat – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 4, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Username, email address, and password — to create and manage your account and to communicate with you if needed (e.g., password resets or important updates).
  • Cookies — to keep you logged in.
  • Basic technical data (like IP address and browser type) — for security and debugging.

We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your login and maintain your session.
  • Send important service-related emails (e.g., Terms updates, security alerts, or password recovery).
  • Improve security and prevent abuse.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access. We use standard security practices (e.g., HTTPS, encrypted passwords, and secure cookies).

4. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. If you delete your account, we remove your data as soon as reasonably possible.

5. Cookies

We use one essential cookie to keep you logged in. It contains a session token only — no personal data — and expires automatically after 30 days. You can clear it anytime in your browser settings.

6. Email Communications

We may send you essential emails related to your account or legal updates (e.g., changes to this Privacy Policy or the Terms of Service). We will not send marketing or promotional emails without your consent.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account or data at any time by emailing: quickchatplatform@hotmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The new version will be posted on this page. If the changes are significant, we may notify you via email.

9. Contact

For any questions, contact us at: quickchatplatform@hotmail.com
